# Features

## 1. People Management
- Track contacts with relationship types, closeness levels, and notes
- Add, edit, and delete people
- Search and filter people

## 2. Interaction Logging
- Log calls, texts, in-person meetings, video chats, and more
- Add notes and photos to interactions
- Schedule future interactions

## 3. Timeline
- See your recent interactions at a glance
- Filter by person, interaction type, or date
- Search interactions

## 4. Insights
- Analytics on interaction frequency, types, and relationship health
- Identify patterns and trends
- Get reminders for upcoming interactions

## 5. Privacy First
- Row-Level Security ensures you only see your own data
- Encrypted data at rest and in transit
- Optional biometric authentication
